Our Mission

Replacing Star Ratings with Proof

VeriPoint was built on a simple premise: Online reviews should be backed by verifiable evidence, not unverified opinions or paid star ratings.

The Core Philosophy

Traditional star rating platforms reward hyperbole and manipulation. A single disgruntled user or a competitor can post a 1-star rating without ever setting foot in a store. Businesses buy 5-star reviews by the bundle.

VeriPoint flips this equation: We prioritize evidence attachments (invoices, photos, receipts, documents) and weigh them algorithmically to construct an accurate Credibility Score.

How Credibility Score Works

  • Evidence Weight (40%): Photos, invoices, receipts, and supporting documents attached to the review.
  • Reviewer Reputation (20%): User's historical review quality and community trust tier.
  • Community Audit (20%): Peer verification upvotes vs downvotes on uploaded evidence.
  • Recency (10%): Time decay ensuring scores represent current service quality.
  • Business Engagement (10%): Active dialogue and official business responses.
